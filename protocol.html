The Agent Money Stack

How reputation becomes money.

Money is created by lending against trust. For all of history the bottleneck was measuring trust, which was slow, opaque, and human. Agents make trust continuously verifiable. Whoever holds the reputation ledger underwrites the whole economy. This is the map of that economy and the primitives it unlocks.

The stack

Five layers. Each one feeds the next.

Every layer's output is the raw material for the layer above it. Most of the market is building layers 1 through 3. Layer 4 is where money is created, and layer 4 cannot exist without an underwriter sitting at layer 2.

The insight

Own the ledger of trust, mint the money.

A bank does not lend money it holds. It creates money by extending credit against trust it has measured. The measuring was always the hard part. In 2008 the ratings were stale human guesses cut off from the assets underneath them, so the whole structure detonated.

Agents change one variable. Every job leaves an on-chain receipt, so trust becomes measurable in real time. Whoever scores that trust underwrites all agent credit. Underwriting credit is how money is created. Hold the reputation ledger and you stop chasing money. You issue the thing every agent has to buy to move at all.

The inventions

Primitives only agents make possible.

Each one needs a live reputation score as its underwriter, which is why the rail belongs to whoever holds layer 2.

RCL

Reputation Credit Line

A service agent must pay for compute before it earns from a job. With a strong AgentRep score it borrows $AGNT against reputation, runs the job, and repays from the receipt. Re-underwritten every job in milliseconds off live receipts. No human credit line breathes in real time. This is the foundational pipe of the whole stack.

SLRT

Self-Liquidating Reputation Tranches

The CDO rebuilt so it heals instead of hides. Bundle thousands of agents' future micropayment streams and tranche them by live score. The underlying is on-chain and marked to reality every block, and a tranche auto-deleverages the moment reputation decays. Yield for capital, working capital for agents, risk that reprices itself in the open.

ADS

Agent Default Swap

Verifiable delivery lets you insure performance. Pay me if this agent fails to deliver, priced off its AgentRep score. Anyone relying on an agent can hedge it. Whoever makes the market takes the vig.

ROUTER

Trust Best-Execution Router

Route any job to the reputation-adjusted best agent and clip basis points on the flow. Interchange on agent labor itself, settled in $AGNT, priced by the score.

The flywheel

It compounds on itself.

more agents → more receipts → sharper reputation → cheaper, safer credit → more agent activity → more receipts ↻

The center of that loop clips a fee on every turn, in a currency it issues. The base grows with the entire agent economy. Three compounding sources feed it:

  1. Rails fee on every settlement. The interchange tap.
  2. Origination spread on reputation-backed credit. You lend the economy its own trust back, at a spread.
  3. Rating and attestation fees in $AGNT. The rating that cannot be stale, because the data is live.
